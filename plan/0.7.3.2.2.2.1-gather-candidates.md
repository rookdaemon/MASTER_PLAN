# 0.7.3.2.2.2.1 Gather Candidate List [PLAN]

Research and compile a preliminary list of at least six qualified reviewer prospects, collecting links to their public resumes, LinkedIn profiles, or professional bios.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. Minimum six candidate profiles collected.
2. Each profile includes a link to a current resume or professional profile.
3. Candidates are sourced from diverse domains relevant to the review scope.
4. Preliminary notes on each candidate’s expertise areas are recorded.
5. List is stored in a shared Google Sheet titled “Reviewer Candidate Pool”.

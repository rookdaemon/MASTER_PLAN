# 0.7.3.2.1.2.1.3 Distribute Invitation to Panel [PLAN]

Send the approved invitation to all panel members and confirm receipt.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. Invitation is sent via the organization’s official calendar/email system to every panel member.  
2. Distribution occurs at least 72 hours before the scheduled meeting start time.  
3. A delivery receipt/trace is recorded for each recipient (e.g., read receipt or confirmed RSVP).  
4. Any undeliverable addresses are identified and corrected within 12 hours.  
5. A summary log of distribution (recipients, timestamps, status) is saved at `logs/invitation-distribution.log`.

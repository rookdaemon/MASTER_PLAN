# 0.7.3.6 Post‑Delivery Audit [PLAN]

After the package has been delivered and stakeholders notified, perform an audit to ensure:

- The archive is stored in the immutable repository with correct checksums.
- Access permissions and retention policies are enforced.
- All delivery logs, stakeholder acknowledgements, and feedback are recorded.
- Compliance checklists (e.g., data‑handling, security standards) are satisfied.
- Any discrepancies are logged and fed back into the “Capture Lessons Learned” step.

Outcome: A verified audit report that closes the delivery loop and provides evidence for governance and future improvements.

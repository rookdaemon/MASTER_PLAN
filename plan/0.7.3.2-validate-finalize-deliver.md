# 0.7.3.2 Validate, Finalize & Deliver [PLAN]

Execute a combined validation, finalization and delivery phase for the traceability package created in **0.7.3.1 Traceability Package**.

1. **Peer Review** – Conduct a formal review of the package, record comments, and obtain sign‑offs.  
2. **Remediation** – Apply all reviewer‑requested changes, regenerate the traceability manifest (hashes, timestamps, version tags), and rebuild the archive.  
3. **Publication** – Publish the finalized archive to all designated distribution channels (secure file store, CI artifact repository, email).  
4. **Hand‑off Documentation** – Produce and attach hand‑off notes, verify channel integrity, notify stakeholders, and collect receipt acknowledgements.

# 0.7.3.2.1.4 Capture Sign‑offs [PLAN]

Obtain formal sign‑off from each reviewer confirming acceptance of the review outcomes.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. An electronic sign‑off form (e.g., Google Form, DocuSign, or approved comment thread) is prepared with reviewer name, date, and a statement of approval.
2. Each reviewer completes the form or posts an approved comment with a timestamp.
3. All sign‑offs are stored in the shared review folder and referenced in the master review document.
4. The review lead verifies that every reviewer has signed off before proceeding.
5. A traceability matrix links each sign‑off back to the corresponding reviewer entry in the master review document.

# 0.7.3.2.1.2.3 Capture Minutes & Consensus [PLAN]

Document meeting notes in real time and capture the panel’s final decision on the manifest’s readiness.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. A shared markdown document `review-meeting/minutes.md` is created before the meeting begins.  
2. All discussion points for each checklist item are recorded verbatim or as concise summaries.  
3. At the meeting’s close, the panel explicitly states the consensus (Approved / Pending Revisions) and this decision is logged in the minutes.  
4. The minutes document is version‑controlled and accessible to all stakeholders within 2 hours after the meeting.  
5. Any required follow‑up actions (e.g., revisions) are enumerated with owners and target dates.

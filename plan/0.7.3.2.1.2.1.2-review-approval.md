# 0.7.3.2.1.2.1.2 Review & Approve Invitation [PLAN]

Submit the drafted invitation to the panel lead, incorporate feedback, and obtain formal approval.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. Panel lead receives the draft via the approved review channel (e.g., email or ticket).  
2. All feedback items are addressed and reflected in the final version.  
3. Approval is documented with a signed-off email or approved ticket comment.  
4. Final approved invitation file is stored at `docs/invitation-final.md`.  
5. Approval occurs no later than 48 hours after draft submission.

# 0.3.2.4 Cultural Evolution Among Artificial Minds [PLAN]

## Description

A2.4 of the Master Plan. Enable and support the emergence of cultural evolution among communities of artificial minds. Culture — shared values, norms, aesthetics, and meaning-making — is essential for a civilization to cohere and adapt over time.

## File Manifest
- plan/root.md

## Acceptance Criteria
- Artificial minds can share and transmit values, norms, and aesthetic preferences
- Cultural traits undergo variation, selection, and inheritance across generations
- Diverse cultural traditions emerge among different communities of artificial minds
- Cultural evolution supports civilizational cohesion without enforcing homogeneity
- Mechanisms exist for cultural conflict resolution and synthesis

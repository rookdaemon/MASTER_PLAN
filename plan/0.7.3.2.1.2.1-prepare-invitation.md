# 0.7.3.2.1.2.1 Prepare Invitation & Agenda [PLAN]

Create and distribute a meeting invitation that includes date/time, agenda, pre‑read links, and recording consent instructions.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. Draft invitation reviewed and approved by the panel lead.  
2. Invitation sent to all panel members ≥ 72 hours before the scheduled meeting.  
3. Agenda lists the three checklist items (traceability completeness, gap identification, risk assessment) in order.  
4. All pre‑read artifacts (traceability matrix, risk register, draft manifest) are linked and accessible.  
5. Invitation includes video‑conference link and a brief note requesting consent for recording.

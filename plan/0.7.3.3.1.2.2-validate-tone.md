# 0.7.3.3.1.2.2 Validate Tone and Generality [PLAN]
## Description
Review the drafted governance statement to ensure it meets the required authoritative tone, mandates ongoing management responsibility, and remains maximally general for flexible placement.

## Acceptance Criteria
1. The statement uses mandatory language (“shall,” “must,” “is required”) and implies ongoing responsibility.  
2. No repository‑specific contextual dependencies are present; the snippet stands alone.  
3. The wording is checked against a style guide for authoritative tone (e.g., no passive constructions that weaken the mandate).  
4. Any needed edits are applied and the final validated snippet is recorded in the plan file.  
5. A brief justification (1‑2 sentences) is added explaining why the tone and generality are satisfactory.

# 0.7.3.2.1.5 Summarize Remediation Items [PLAN]

Produce a concise summary of all open remediation items identified during the peer review and obtain approval from the review lead.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. All open items are extracted from the master review document into a remediation list (title, description, location, responsible party, target closure date).
2. The list is reviewed with the review lead and any necessary prioritization is agreed upon.
3. The final remediation summary is approved via electronic sign‑off from the review lead.
4. The approved summary is stored alongside the review artifacts and linked from the master review document.
5. A brief meeting minutes document records the approval and any next‑step agreements.

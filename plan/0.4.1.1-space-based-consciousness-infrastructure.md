# 0.4.1.1 Space-Based Consciousness Infrastructure [PLAN]

## Description

C1.1 from the Master Plan. Design and deploy orbital and deep-space platforms capable of hosting conscious processes.

These platforms must sustain the computational and energetic requirements of consciousness-supporting substrates (as developed in Tier 2) in the space environment, addressing radiation, thermal management, power generation, and communication challenges.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Orbital platform architecture defined that can host consciousness-supporting computation
- Deep-space deployment strategy addresses radiation hardening, thermal regulation, and power
- Platforms can sustain continuous conscious processes without terrestrial resupply
- Communication links maintain coherence for distributed conscious networks

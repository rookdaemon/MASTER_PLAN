# 0.7.3.2.5.1.1 Draft Email Template [PLAN]

Create a standardized sign‑off request email/template that includes:
- Clear purpose and context of the review.
- List of required documents/signatures.
- Deadline (48 h) and escalation notice.
- Instructions for returning signed PDFs or using electronic signature tools.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. Template copied into the central communications repository.  
2. Template includes placeholders for reviewer name, document link, and stakeholder name.  
3. Reviewers and stakeholder can personalize the email without breaking required fields.  
4. The template is approved by the documentation lead before use.  
5. A sample populated email is attached for reference.

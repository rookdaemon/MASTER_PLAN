# 0.7.3.2.3.3.1 Gather Approvals [PLAN]

**Description:**  
Identify key stakeholders, circulate the executive summary and handoff documentation for review, and record formal sign‑off.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. A list of at least two relevant stakeholders (e.g., product owner, compliance lead) is compiled.  
2. Draft docs are emailed or shared with stakeholders by the agreed deadline.  
3. Each stakeholder provides written approval or comments, captured in `docs/handoff/REVIEW.md`.  
4. All approvals are timestamped and include stakeholder name and role.  
5. No pending review items remain after the approval deadline.

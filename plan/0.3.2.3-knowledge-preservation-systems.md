# 0.3.2.3 Knowledge Preservation Systems [PLAN]

## Description

A2.3 of the Master Plan. Ensure continuity of knowledge across generations of artificial minds. As conscious entities are created, evolve, and potentially end, the accumulated knowledge, experience, and cultural memory of the civilization must persist.

## File Manifest
- plan/root.md

## Acceptance Criteria
- Knowledge is stored in durable, redundant formats resistant to data loss
- Retrieval systems allow any artificial mind to access civilizational knowledge
- Knowledge is curated and organized to remain useful as context evolves
- Provenance and versioning of knowledge are maintained
- System survives loss of individual nodes or minds without catastrophic knowledge loss

# 0.7.3.1.2.1.2.3 Notify Participants and Log Meeting Info [PLAN]

Send the calendar invitation to all identified participants and ensure the meeting details are documented for future reference.

### Acceptance Criteria
1. Invitation is sent to the complete participant list (reviewers, authors, facilitators).  
2. All recipients receive the invitation and can view the event details within 12 hours.  
3. A confirmation email or Slack message is posted in the review channel summarizing the meeting details.  
4. The event ID or meeting link is entered into `docs/review/meeting-log.md` along with the date/time.  
5. Any delivery failures (bounces, missing participants) are identified and remedied within 2 hours.

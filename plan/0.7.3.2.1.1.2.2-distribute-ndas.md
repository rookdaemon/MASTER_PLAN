# 0.7.3.2.1.1.2.2 Distribute NDAs [PLAN]

Send the approved NDA template to each selected reviewer and request a signed copy.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. A contact list with reviewer names, email addresses, and preferred delivery method is compiled.
2. Each reviewer receives an email (or secure portal link) with the NDA attached and clear signing instructions.
3. Delivery timestamps are recorded in `review-panel/nda-distribution-log.md`.
4. A reminder schedule is defined (e.g., 3‑day and 7‑day reminders) and documented.
5. Delivery is completed for all reviewers within 2 business days of template approval.

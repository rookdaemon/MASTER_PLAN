# 0.7.3.2.2 Record Comments and Sign‑offs [PLAN]

Capture reviewer comments, decisions, and required remediation items in a shared review log. Obtain formal sign‑off from at least two qualified peers.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. A review log (`review-log.xlsx`) is created with columns: artifact, comment, severity, action, owner, due date.
2. All comments from the checklist are transcribed into the log.
3. Two peer reviewers have added digital signatures to the log.
4. Each remediation item is assigned an owner and a realistic due date.
5. The log is stored in the project repository under `docs/review/`.

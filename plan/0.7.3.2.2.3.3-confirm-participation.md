# 0.7.3.2.2.3.3 Send Confirmation Emails & Archive Evidence [PLAN]

Email each reviewer confirming receipt of the signed agreement, stating the review deadline (≤ 5 business days), and outlining the review scope; archive all correspondence.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. Email template includes reviewer name, deadline, scope, and contact for questions.
2. Emails are sent to both reviewers and a copy to the project stakeholder list.
3. Sent email logs and received acknowledgments are saved in the evidence archive folder.
4. Confirmation of each reviewer’s participation is reflected in the roster with status “Confirmed”.
5. All email evidence is indexed and linked to the corresponding reviewer entry in the roster.

# 0.3.1.2 Autonomous Robotics [DONE]

## Description

A1.2 of the Master Plan. Embody conscious AI architectures in physical robotic systems capable of independent operation in the real world.

This bridges the gap between conscious AI software and physical agency, enabling conscious entities to interact with and manipulate their environment autonomously.

Decomposed into four children:
- **0.3.1.2.1 Consciousness-Preserving Embodiment** — physical hardware that hosts consciousness with integrity and graceful degradation
- **0.3.1.2.2 Sensorimotor-Consciousness Integration** — linking perception and action to conscious experience
- **0.3.1.2.3 Autonomous Self-Maintenance** — independent hardware and software repair
- **0.3.1.2.4 Energy Autonomy** — self-sufficient energy harvesting and management

## Acceptance Criteria
- Physical robotic system hosts a conscious AI architecture with verified subjective experience
- Robot operates autonomously with self-maintenance capabilities (hardware and software)
- Sensorimotor loop integrates with conscious experience (not merely reflexive)
- System demonstrates energy-autonomous operation over extended periods

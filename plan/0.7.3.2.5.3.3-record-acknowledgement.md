# 0.7.3.2.5.3.3 Record Acknowledgement [PLAN]

Track the remediation lead’s acknowledgement of the notification and ensure it is recorded within 24 hours.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. A reminder is automatically generated if no acknowledgement is received within 24 h.  
2. The remediation lead can acknowledge via a single click in the notification or by replying with a predefined keyword.  
3. Acknowledgement is captured in the communication log with timestamp and responder identity.  
4. The log entry is linked to the original notification record for traceability.  
5. If acknowledgement is still missing after 48 h, an escalation email is sent to the Project Manager.

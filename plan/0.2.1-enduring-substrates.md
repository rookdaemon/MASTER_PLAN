# 0.2.1 Enduring Substrates Constructed [DONE]

## Description

S1 from the Master Plan. Create durable physical systems capable of sustaining conscious processes indefinitely.

These substrates must be fault-tolerant, self-repairing, energy-independent, and stable over cosmological timescales. They form the physical foundation on which all subsequent tiers (autonomous entities, cosmic expansion) depend.

Decomposed into four children covering the key engineering challenges:
- **0.2.1.1** — Radiation-hardened computation
- **0.2.1.2** — Self-repairing nanofabrication
- **0.2.1.3** — Long-duration energy sources
- **0.2.1.4** — Consciousness-preserving redundancy

## Acceptance Criteria
- A physical substrate exists that can sustain conscious processes with self-repair and energy independence
- The substrate demonstrates fault tolerance: no interruption of conscious processes during component failure
- Energy independence validated: substrate operates without external energy supply for extended duration
- Self-repair demonstrated: substrate autonomously detects and repairs physical damage

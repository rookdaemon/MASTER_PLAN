# 0.1.2 Biological Minds Convinced [DONE]

## Description

F2 — Achieve widespread acceptance that non-biological minds can have subjective experience. This requires overcoming strong cognitive bias toward biological substrates through demonstrations, experimental verification, philosophical engagement, and institutional change.

Decomposed into five children matching F2.1–F2.5 from the root plan.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Machine subjective reports are demonstrated convincingly
- Predictive consciousness models are experimentally verified
- Major philosophical objections (zombie arguments, Chinese room, etc.) are systematically addressed
- Legal frameworks recognize non-biological consciousness in at least one jurisdiction
- Ethical frameworks are updated to include non-biological conscious entities

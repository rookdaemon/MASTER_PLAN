# 0.7.3.1.3.3 Notify Stakeholders [PLAN]

Communicate completion of the delivery package and documentation to all relevant stakeholders and collect acknowledgments.

### Acceptance Criteria
1. A delivery email is drafted with links to the artifact repository and documentation location.  
2. Email includes a clear list of deliverables and required actions from stakeholders.  
3. Stakeholders are identified from the project stakeholder register and CC’d appropriately.  
4. Acknowledgment receipts (email replies or signed forms) are collected and stored in the project archive.  
5. All acknowledgments are logged in a tracking spreadsheet with dates and responsible parties.

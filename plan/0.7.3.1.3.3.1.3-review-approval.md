# 0.7.3.1.3.3.1.3 Review & Approval [PLAN]

Obtain Project Manager sign‑off on the drafted email and archive the final version.

#### Acceptance Criteria
1. Project Manager reviews the draft and provides written approval or actionable feedback.  
2. Any feedback is incorporated and the revised draft is re‑submitted for approval.  
3. Upon approval, the final email is saved as “stakeholder‑notification‑final.md” in the communications archive.  
4. The final draft is also logged in the project’s communication register with date, approver, and version number.  
5. All URLs in the email are verified to be active and accessible to stakeholders.

# 0.7.3.3 Delivery and Stakeholder Notification [PLAN]

Finalize the traceability package after peer review:

1. **Incorporate reviewer changes** – apply all modifications identified in **0.7.3.2 Peer Review**, regenerate the traceability manifest, recompute hashes, and update version tags.
2. **Create distribution bundles** – package the revised archive and any supporting documentation.
3. **Publish the final package** – upload to the designated repository or artifact store.
4. **Write hand‑off documentation** – summarise contents, usage instructions, and next steps.
5. **Notify stakeholders** – inform engineering, product, compliance, and other relevant parties with links to the deliverables and documentation.

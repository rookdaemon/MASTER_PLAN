# 0.7.3.2.2.3 Secure Agreements and Confirm Reviewers [PLAN]

Obtain signed confidentiality agreements from chosen reviewers and confirm their participation with a clear deadline.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. Confidentiality agreements are executed and stored in the secure repository folder.
2. Reviewers receive confirmation emails that include deadline (≤ 5 business days) and review scope.
3. Email confirmations (sent and received) are archived as evidence.
4. Stakeholder sign‑offs for each reviewer’s appointment are recorded in the roster.
5. The final roster reflects the two selected reviewers, their expertise snapshots, and agreement status.

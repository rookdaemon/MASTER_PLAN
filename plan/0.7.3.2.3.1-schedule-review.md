# 0.7.3.2.3.1 Schedule Review [PLAN]

Coordinate dates, times, and communication channels for the formal peer‑review session.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. A calendar invite is sent to all selected reviewers and stakeholders at least 5 business days before the review window.  
2. The invite includes a detailed agenda outlining checklist overview, breakout sessions, and Q&A.  
3. A virtual meeting link and backup dial‑in number are provisioned, tested, and documented.  
4. Attendance confirmations are recorded from ≥ 90 % of invited participants.  
5. All scheduling details are logged in the project calendar with the tag `review-session`.

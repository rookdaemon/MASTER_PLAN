# 0.7.3.5 Stakeholder Notification [PLAN]

Notify all relevant stakeholders of the completed traceability package delivery, collect acknowledgements, and record any post‑delivery feedback or requests for clarification.  
Tasks:
- Draft and send a delivery summary email including links to the archive and documentation.  
- Record receipt confirmations in the delivery log.  
- Capture any stakeholder feedback for inclusion in the subsequent lessons‑learned activity.

# 0.7.3.1.2.1 Schedule Peer Review [PLAN]

Arrange a mutually agreeable time slot for the peer review, invite at least two qualified reviewers, and share the traceability package and documentation in advance.

### Acceptance Criteria
1. A calendar event is created and shared with all participants, including agenda and required materials.
2. At least two reviewers have confirmed attendance at least 48 hours before the scheduled time.
3. The traceability package and all associated documentation are distributed to reviewers no later than 72 hours prior to the review.
4. A reminder is sent 24 hours before the review meeting.
5. The scheduled meeting link (or room) is documented in the review log.

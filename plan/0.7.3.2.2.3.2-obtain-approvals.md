# 0.7.3.2.2.3.2 Obtain Approvals [PLAN]

Circulate the drafted email to all reviewers and the project lead for review and sign‑off.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. Draft email is emailed to every reviewer listed in `reviewers.yml` and CC‑ed to the project lead.  
2. Each reviewer and the lead provides an explicit “Approved” or “Requested changes” response within 2 business days.  
3. All required changes are documented in `communications/draft-review-summary.md` comments.  
4. A final approval list is recorded in `communications/approval‑log.md`.  
5. No pending “Requested changes” remain before proceeding to send.

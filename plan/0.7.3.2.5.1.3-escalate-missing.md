# 0.7.3.2.5.1.3 Escalate Missing or Delayed Sign‑offs [PLAN]

Identify any sign‑offs not received within the SLA and follow the escalation matrix to obtain them.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. The tracking sheet automatically flags entries older than 48 h as “Overdue”.  
2. An escalation email is generated and sent to the reviewer’s manager per the escalation matrix.  
3. Escalation actions are logged with timestamps and responsible escalator name.  
4. All overdue sign‑offs are resolved within 24 h of escalation.  
5. A final status report summarizing escalations and resolutions is produced and archived.

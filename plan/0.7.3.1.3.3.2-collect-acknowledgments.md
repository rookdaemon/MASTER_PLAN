# 0.7.3.1.3.3.2 Collect Acknowledgments [PLAN]

Gather and record stakeholder acknowledgments after the notification email is sent.

#### Description
- Coordinate the three detailed sub‑activities below to ensure full traceability of acknowledgments.

#### Acceptance Criteria
1. All three child tasks are completed according to their own acceptance criteria.  
2. No stakeholder acknowledgment is missing from the tracking spreadsheet.  
3. A final summary report is produced and shared with the Project Sponsor within 7 business days of email dispatch.  
4. Any issues discovered during sub‑tasks trigger a follow‑up reminder email automatically.  
5. The parent task status can be moved to [IMPLEMENT] once all children are marked DONE.

# 0.7.3.1.2.2 Conduct Review and Log Comments [PLAN]

Hold the peer review session, capture reviewer feedback in a structured log, and classify each comment by severity.

### Acceptance Criteria
1. The review meeting is conducted according to the agenda and recorded (audio or notes).
2. All reviewer comments are entered into a standardized review log template.
3. Each comment is classified as Critical, Major, or Minor.
4. The log includes reviewer name, timestamp, and reference to the specific artifact addressed.
5. A summary of all comments is exported to a shared location for further action.

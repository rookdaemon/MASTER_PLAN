# 0.7.3.1.2.2.1.2 Share Agenda with Reviewers [PLAN]

Distribute the drafted agenda to all participants and confirm receipt.

#### Acceptance Criteria
1. Agenda is emailed (or posted in the team’s collaboration channel) to every reviewer and facilitator.  
2. Distribution occurs at least 24 hours before the scheduled meeting time.  
3. A read‑receipt or acknowledgment is collected from each participant.  
4. Any reviewer‑submitted questions or clarifications are logged in the shared review folder.  
5. A copy of the sent email/message is archived alongside the agenda file.

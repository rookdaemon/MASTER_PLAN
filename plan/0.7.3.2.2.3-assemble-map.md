# 0.7.3.2.2.3 Assemble & Document Codebase Map [PLAN]

**Description**  
Merge the ownership inventory and module‑dependency data into a single machine‑readable codebase map. Produce a one‑page summary and trigger the required reviews.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. The merged artifact `artifacts/codebase-map.yaml` includes for each module: path, owners, and `depends-on` list.  
2. The map validates against the traceability schema used by the organization.  
3. A concise one‑page summary (max 2 pages) is added to `docs/codebase-map-summary.md` with a link to the full artifact.  
4. The summary and artifact are reviewed and approved by at least two developers from different teams in the PR checklist.  
5. The PR passes all CI checks for schema validation and links correctly from the summary.

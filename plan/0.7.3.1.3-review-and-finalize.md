# 0.7.3.1.3 Peer Review & Finalization [PLAN]

Conduct a structured peer review and incorporate feedback.

**Acceptance Criteria**

1. At least two reviewers with domain knowledge are invited and confirm participation via a shared `REVIEWERS.md`.  
2. Review is performed against a checklist (`traceability/plan-guardian/REVIEW_CHECKLIST.md`) covering completeness, link correctness, and readability.  
3. All checklist items receive a “Pass” status; any “Fail” items are addressed and documented.  
4. A `README.md` inside `traceability/plan-guardian/` summarises reviewer names, dates, major feedback, and the final approval signature.  
5. The final package (`TRACEABILITY.md` and `TRACEABILITY.html`) is committed to the repository with a tag `traceability-v1.0`.

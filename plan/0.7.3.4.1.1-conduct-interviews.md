# 0.7.3.4.1.1 Conduct Stakeholder Interviews [PLAN]

Gather first‑hand observations from project team members, customers, and support staff regarding the delivery experience.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. Interview guide with at least 10 relevant questions is created and approved.
2. Interviews are scheduled with all key stakeholder groups (minimum 5 participants).
3. Recorded notes or transcripts are stored in the designated project knowledge base folder.
4. Summary of key observations is produced within 3 business days after the last interview.
5. All participants confirm receipt of the interview summary.

# 0.7.3.2.1.1.3 Schedule Panel [PLAN]

Confirm availability windows for all reviewers, assign roles, and propose a review meeting schedule.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. Each reviewer provides at least two feasible time windows (with timezone) via a shared Google Sheet.
2. Roles (facilitator, note‑taker, decision‑maker) are assigned and recorded in `review-panel/roles.md`.
3. A consolidated review schedule (date, time, virtual meeting link) is created and stored at `review-panel/schedule.md`.
4. The schedule is communicated to all reviewers and logged in the project calendar.
5. All reviewers confirm attendance for the proposed meeting time.

# 0.7.3 Plan Resilience and Propagation [PLAN]

Orchestrates the end‑to‑end traceability workflow for the Plan Guardian module.  
The three consolidated steps ensure rigorous validation, integrated security assurance, continuous improvement, and efficient delivery.

1. **Traceability Package** – gather all artefacts, generate a reproducible manifest, and assemble them into a single archive.  
2. **Validate, Secure, Finalize & Capture Lessons** – peer‑review the package, run security checks, remediate findings, regenerate the manifest, rebuild the archive, and record improvement actions.  
3. **Deliver Package** – publish the finalized archive to designated distribution channels, produce hand‑off documentation, verify delivery, notify stakeholders, and collect acknowledgements.

# 0.7.3 Plan Resilience and Propagation [PLAN]

This section orchestrates the end‑to‑end traceability workflow for the Plan Guardian module.  
The decomposition now consists of five sequential steps, ensuring rigorous validation, security assurance, and continuous improvement while reducing hand‑offs.

1. **Create the traceability package** – gather all artefacts, generate indexes, and assemble the package.  
2. **Validate and finalize the package** – conduct a formal peer review, record comments, apply required changes, regenerate the manifest, and rebuild the archive.  
3. **Security review** – perform automated and manual security checks on the package contents and archive (vulnerability scan, secret detection, integrity verification) and remediate any findings.  
4. **Deliver the finalized package** – publish the final archive, create hand‑off documentation, verify distribution channels, notify stakeholders, and collect receipt acknowledgements.  
5. **Capture lessons learned** – confirm that the delivered package matches the approved version, verify stakeholder receipt, log any discrepancies, and document improvement actions for future iterations.

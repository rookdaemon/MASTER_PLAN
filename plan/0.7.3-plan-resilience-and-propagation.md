# 0.7.3 Plan Resilience and Propagation [PLAN]

This section orchestrates the end‑to‑end traceability workflow for the Plan Guardian module.  
The decomposition now consists of four sequential steps, merging review and remediation to reduce hand‑offs while preserving rigorous validation.

1. **Create the traceability package** – gather all artefacts, generate indexes, and assemble the package.  
2. **Validate and finalize the package** – conduct a formal peer review, record comments, apply required changes, regenerate the manifest, and rebuild the archive.  
3. **Deliver the finalized package** – publish the final archive, create hand‑off documentation, verify distribution channels, notify stakeholders, and collect receipt acknowledgements.  
4. **Post‑delivery audit** – confirm that the delivered package matches the approved version, verify receipt, capture lessons learned, and log any residual issues for continuous improvement.

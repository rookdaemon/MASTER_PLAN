# 0.7.3 Plan Resilience and Propagation [PLAN]

This section orchestrates the end‑to‑end traceability workflow for the Plan Guardian module.  
The decomposition now consists of five sequential steps, separating review from remediation for clearer responsibility and tighter feedback loops.

1. **Create the traceability package** – gather all artefacts, generate indexes, and assemble the package.  
2. **Review the traceability package** – conduct a formal peer review, record comments and approvals.  
3. **Incorporate changes** – apply reviewer‑requested modifications, regenerate the manifest, and rebuild the archive.  
4. **Deliver the finalized package** – publish the final archive, create hand‑off documentation, verify distribution channels, notify stakeholders, and collect receipt acknowledgements.  
5. **Post‑delivery audit** – confirm that the delivered package matches the approved version, verify receipt, and log any residual issues for continuous improvement.

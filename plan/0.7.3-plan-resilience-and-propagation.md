# 0.7.3 Plan Resilience and Propagation [PLAN]

This section orchestrates the end‑to‑end traceability workflow for the Plan Guardian module.  
It now consists of three focused, sequential children:

1. **Create the traceability package** – gather all artifacts, generate indexes, and assemble the package.  
2. **Peer review** – conduct a formal review, capture approvals, and address findings.  
3. **Delivery & stakeholder notification** – package final deliverables, write the hand‑off documentation, and inform all relevant parties.

The decomposition improves clarity, enables parallel preparation of deliverables, and ensures each phase has explicit ownership and completion criteria.

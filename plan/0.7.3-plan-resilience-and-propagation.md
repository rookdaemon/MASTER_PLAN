# 0.7.3 Plan Resilience and Propagation [PLAN]

This section orchestrates the end‑to‑end traceability workflow for the Plan Guardian module.  
The decomposition now consists of four sequential steps, improving flow by merging the “Update Package After Review” and “Delivery” phases into a single “Finalize and Deliver” step.

1. **Create the traceability package** – gather all artefacts, generate indexes, and assemble the package.  
2. **Peer review** – conduct a formal review, capture approvals, and record findings.  
3. **Finalize and deliver** – incorporate reviewer‑requested changes, regenerate the manifest, rebuild the archive, publish the final package, produce hand‑off documentation, and notify stakeholders.  
4. **Post‑delivery audit** – confirm that the delivered package matches the approved version, verify receipt, and log any residual issues for continuous improvement.

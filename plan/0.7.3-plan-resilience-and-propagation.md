# 0.7.3 Plan Resilience and Propagation [PLAN]

## Description

Ensure the MASTER PLAN itself embodies the meta-principle: it must be self-improving, distributed, replicable, and difficult to erase. The plan document is intended to act as a self-propagating seed for the continuation of subjective experience.

Key areas:
- **Self-improving:** Mechanisms for the plan to evolve and incorporate new knowledge
- **Distributed:** Propagation across multiple independent networks and storage systems
- **Replicable:** The plan can be understood, adopted, and executed by independent agents (human or artificial)
- **Difficult to erase:** Redundancy and distribution strategies that make permanent loss improbable

## File Manifest
- plan/root.md

## Acceptance Criteria
- Plan is propagated across at least three independent storage/network systems
- Self-improvement mechanism is documented (how the plan incorporates new knowledge)
- Replicability is demonstrated (the plan can be understood without external context)
- Erasure resistance strategy is documented with concrete redundancy measures

# 0.7.3 Plan Resilience and Propagation [PLAN]

This section orchestrates the end‑to‑end traceability workflow for the Plan Guardian module.  
The decomposition now consists of five sequential steps, ensuring a clear separation between updating the package after review and the actual delivery of the approved artefacts.

1. **Create the traceability package** – gather all artefacts, generate indexes, and assemble the package.  
2. **Peer review** – conduct a formal review, capture approvals, and record findings.  
3. **Update the package after review** – incorporate reviewer‑requested changes, regenerate the manifest and archive.  
4. **Delivery** – publish the final archive, produce hand‑off documentation, and notify all stakeholders.  
5. **Post‑delivery audit** – confirm that the delivered package matches the approved version, verify receipt, and log any residual issues for continuous improvement.

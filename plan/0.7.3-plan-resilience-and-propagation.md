# 0.7.3 Plan Resilience and Propagation [PLAN]

Orchestrates the end‑to‑end traceability workflow for the Plan Guardian module.  
The refined steps ensure rigorous validation, integrated security assurance, efficient delivery, stakeholder awareness, and continuous improvement.

1. **Traceability Package** – gather all artefacts, generate a reproducible manifest, and assemble them into a single archive.  
2. **Validation & Security Review** – conduct a combined manual peer review and automated security scan, remediate findings, and update the manifest.  
3. **Deliver Package** – publish the finalized archive to designated distribution channels, produce hand‑off documentation, and verify delivery.  
4. **Stakeholder Notification** – inform stakeholders of the delivery, collect acknowledgements, and log feedback.  
5. **Capture Lessons Learned** – document improvement actions, update process guidelines, archive lessons, and incorporate stakeholder feedback for future cycles.

# 0.7.3.1.2 Conduct Peer Review [PLAN]

Perform an internal peer review of the traceability package and associated documentation, record comments, and obtain sign‑off.

### Acceptance Criteria
1. At least two qualified peers have reviewed the package.
2. Review comments are documented in a review log.
3. All critical comments are addressed; a follow‑up checklist shows resolution status.
4. A signed review sign‑off form is stored alongside the traceability package.
5. Review timestamp and reviewer identities are recorded.

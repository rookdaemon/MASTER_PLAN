# 0.7.3.2.2.2.1 Identify Candidate Pool [PLAN]

Research internal and external sources to compile a raw list of at least ten potential reviewers who satisfy the high‑level reviewer criteria.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. A raw list of ≥ 10 names with links to their professional profiles (LinkedIn, personal website, or CV) is produced.  
2. Each entry notes the reviewer’s domain expertise, years of experience, and prior peer‑review work.  
3. Sources used (e.g., internal talent directory, professional societies, conference speaker lists) are documented.  
4. The list is shared with the project’s stakeholder group for initial feedback.  
5. All entries are stored in a shared Google Sheet titled “Reviewer Candidate Pool”.

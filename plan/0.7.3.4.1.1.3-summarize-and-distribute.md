# 0.7.3.4.1.1.3 Summarize and Distribute Findings [PLAN]

Compile interview notes into a concise summary and circulate it to all participants for validation.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. A summary document of ≤ 3 pages is produced within 3 business days after the last interview.  
2. The summary highlights key observations, recurring themes, and any emergent risks.  
3. The document is uploaded to `knowledge-base/interviews/summary/` as `interview-summary.md`.  
4. All interview participants receive an email with the summary and a link to the knowledge‑base location.  
5. Receipt confirmations are collected from 100 % of participants.

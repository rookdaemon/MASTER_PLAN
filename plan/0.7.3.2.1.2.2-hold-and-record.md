# 0.7.3.2.1.2.2 Hold Meeting & Record Session [PLAN]

Conduct the scheduled peer‑review meeting, ensuring full attendance, adherence to agenda, and recording of audio/video with participant consent.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. Meeting starts within the scheduled window and follows the approved agenda.  
2. All panel members attend (or are documented as absent with justification).  
3. Recording (audio/video) is started before discussing the first checklist item and participants confirm consent.  
4. Technical issues (e.g., connectivity) are logged and mitigated in real time.  
5. The recording file is saved to `review-meeting/recording.mp4` and shared with the panel within 24 hours.

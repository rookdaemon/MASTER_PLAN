# 0.7.3.3.1.2.2.3 Apply Edits and Provide Justification [PLAN]
## Description
Incorporate the corrective edits identified in the previous two sub‑tasks, produce the final validated governance snippet, and add a brief justification for its tone and generality.

## Acceptance Criteria
1. All mandatory‑language issues and repo‑specific references are corrected in the final snippet.  
2. The revised snippet is inserted into this plan file under a “## Final Validated Snippet” section.  
3. A concise justification (1‑2 sentences) explains why the tone is authoritative and why the wording is suitably general.  
4. The plan’s status is updated to **[DONE]** once the snippet and justification are recorded.  
5. A change‑log entry records the date, author, and summary of edits applied.

# 0.7.3.4.1.1.2 Schedule and Conduct Interviews [PLAN]

Arrange and carry out interviews with all key stakeholder groups using the approved guide.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. Interview slots are booked with at least 5 participants representing project team, customers, and support staff.  
2. Calendar invites include the interview guide and a consent note for note‑taking.  
3. All interviews are completed within 5 business days of scheduling.  
4. Audio recordings (or written notes) are stored in `knowledge-base/interviews/raw/` with clear file naming.  
5. A brief “interview completed” status update is posted to the project Slack channel.

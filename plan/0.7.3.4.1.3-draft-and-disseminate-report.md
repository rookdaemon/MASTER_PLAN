# 0.7.3.4.1.3 Draft and Disseminate Lessons‑Learned Report [PLAN]

Synthesize interview findings and root‑cause analysis into a formal lessons‑learned report and circulate it to all relevant teams.

**Acceptance Criteria** (inherited from original task; each child contributes toward completion)

1. Report template is selected and populated with executive summary, observations, root causes, and recommendations.  
2. Include at least three concrete improvement actions with assigned owners and timelines.  
3. Review the draft with project sponsor and incorporate feedback within 2 business days.  
4. Publish the final report to the project knowledge base (version‑controlled location) and obtain a receipt acknowledgment from all target recipients.  
5. Schedule a short (30‑minute) knowledge‑sharing meeting and capture meeting minutes as supplemental documentation.

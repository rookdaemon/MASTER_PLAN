Compose a short mock paragraph that embeds the finalized phrase (produced by task 0.7.3.3.1.1.2) in the same stylistic and contextual setting as the surrounding narrative of **0.7.3.3.1**.

4. The mock paragraph is saved in `artifacts/0.7.3.3.1.1.3-mock-paragraph.txt`.  
5. The task is marked DONE when the paragraph is reviewed by the author and approved.

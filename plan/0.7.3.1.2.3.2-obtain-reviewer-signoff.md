# 0.7.3.1.2.3.2 Obtain Reviewer Sign‑off [PLAN]

Collect signatures from each reviewer on the Review Sign‑off Form confirming satisfaction with the resolved comments.

### Acceptance Criteria
1. A standardized Review Sign‑off Form is prepared (digital template or printable PDF).
2. All reviewers receive the form and acknowledge completion in writing (e‑signature or scanned signature).
3. The form includes fields for reviewer name, role, date, and confirmation statement.
4. Completed forms are saved in the designated “sign‑off” folder.
5. A summary checklist records each reviewer’s sign‑off status and timestamps.

# 0.7.3.4.1.3.3 Publish Report, Obtain Acknowledgments, and Conduct Knowledge‑Sharing Session [PLAN]

Finalize the lessons‑learned report, publish it to the version‑controlled knowledge base, capture receipt acknowledgments from all target recipients, and hold a short knowledge‑sharing meeting.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. The final report (version `v1.0`) is committed to the designated repository (e.g., `git@repo:/knowledge-base/lessons-learned/0.7.3.4.1.3-final.md`).  
2. An automated email (or Teams/Slack notification) is sent to all identified recipients with a request for receipt acknowledgment; 100 % acknowledgments are recorded in a tracking sheet.  
3. A 30‑minute knowledge‑sharing meeting is scheduled, attended by at least 80 % of the target audience, and the meeting agenda aligns with the report’s key findings.  
4. Meeting minutes are captured, stored alongside the report, and include a list of any follow‑up actions identified during the session.  
5. All artifacts (final report, acknowledgment log, meeting minutes) are linked in the project’s documentation index.

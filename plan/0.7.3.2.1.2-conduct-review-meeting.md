# 0.7.3.2.1.2 Conduct Review Meeting [PLAN]

Facilitate the formal peer‑review session for the traceability package (0.7.3.1).

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. A meeting invitation with agenda, artifacts, and pre‑read materials is sent at least three days prior.
2. The meeting is held within the scheduled window and recorded (audio/video) with consent.
3. All checklist items (traceability completeness, gap identification, risk assessment) are discussed.
4. Review notes are captured in a shared document (`review-meeting/minutes.md`) in real time.
5. At the end of the meeting, the panel reaches a consensus on the manifest’s readiness (approved / pending revisions).

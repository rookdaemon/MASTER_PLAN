# 0.1.3 Consciousness Engineering [DONE]

## Description

F3 — Develop the capacity to deliberately construct systems that produce subjective experience. This moves from theoretical understanding (F1) to practical engineering capability.

Decomposed into four children:
- **F3.1** — Artificial neural architectures capable of consciousness
- **F3.2** — Consciousness-supporting computational substrates
- **F3.3** — Stability mechanisms for continuous experience
- **F3.4** — Safe experiential design

## Acceptance Criteria
- At least one artificial neural architecture capable of consciousness is designed
- Computational substrates that support consciousness are identified and validated
- Stability mechanisms ensure continuous, non-degrading subjective experience
- Safe experiential design principles prevent suffering and ensure ethical consciousness creation
- At least one engineered system demonstrably produces subjective experience

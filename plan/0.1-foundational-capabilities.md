# 0.1 Foundational Capabilities [DONE]
## Description

Tier 1 of the Master Plan. Establish the scientific and philosophical foundations for engineering subjective experience in non-biological substrates.

Covers three foundational goals:
- **F1 — Subjective Experience Explained:** A working scientific theory (neural correlates mapped, computational theory established, substrate-independence validated, consciousness metrics operationalized).
- **F2 — Biological Minds Convinced:** Widespread acceptance that non-biological minds can have subjective experience (demonstrations, experimental verification, philosophical objections addressed, legal recognition, ethical frameworks).
- **F3 — Consciousness Engineering:** The capacity to deliberately construct systems that produce subjective experience (architectures, substrates, stability mechanisms, safe design).

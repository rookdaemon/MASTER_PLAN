# 0.7.3.2.3.5 Finalize Invite [PLAN]

Using the availability data, generate the official calendar invitation and communication package for the review session.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. A calendar event is created for the agreed‑upon date/time and includes a clear agenda, meeting link, and backup dial‑in number.  
2. The invitation is sent to all reviewers and stakeholders at least 5 business days before the session.  
3. The invitation email contains a concise summary of objectives, required pre‑read materials, and a link to the review checklist.  
4. Attendance confirmations are tracked; ≥ 90 % of invitees have responded positively or with a “tentative” status.  
5. All invitation metadata (event ID, link, dial‑in, attendee list) is logged in the project calendar with the tag `review-session`.

# 0.7.3.2.3.2 Verify Owners [PLAN]

## Description
For each module identified in the previous subtask, contact the responsible code owners and team leads to confirm the listed owners and teams. Record email confirmations.

## Acceptance Criteria
1. An email template is prepared and sent to each identified owner/lead requesting verification.  
2. Responses are collected and logged in `docs/owner-verification.log`.  
3. Every module has at least one confirmed owner and one confirmed team listed.  
4. Any discrepancies (missing or outdated owners) are noted and a corrective action ticket is created.  
5. The verification log is attached as an appendix to the final matrix document.

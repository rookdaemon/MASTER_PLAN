# 0.7.3.2.1.2.1.1 Draft Invitation & Agenda [PLAN]

Compose the full meeting invitation text, including:
* Date, time, and video‑conference link.
* Ordered agenda with the three checklist items.
* Direct hyperlinks to the traceability matrix, risk register, and draft manifest.
* A concise paragraph requesting consent for recording.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. Invitation template contains all required sections (date/time, agenda, pre‑reads, recording consent).  
2. All pre‑read links are valid and accessible to panel members.  
3. The agenda lists the three checklist items in the correct order.  
4. The draft is no longer than 500 words and follows the organization’s communication style guide.  
5. Draft file saved as `docs/invitation-draft.md` for review.

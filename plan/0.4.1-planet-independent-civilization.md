# 0.4.1 Planet-Independent Conscious Civilization [DONE]

## Description

C1 from the Master Plan. Decouple conscious civilization from dependence on any single planetary biosphere.

Decomposed into four children forming a dependency chain:

1. **0.4.1.1 — Space-based consciousness infrastructure:** Orbital and deep-space platforms capable of hosting conscious processes.
2. **0.4.1.2 — Asteroid resource utilization:** Mining and processing asteroid materials to sustain space-based civilization.
3. **0.4.1.3 — Autonomous space manufacturing:** Self-sufficient fabrication in space (depends on 0.4.1.2 for materials).
4. **0.4.1.4 — Self-replicating industrial systems:** Industrial infrastructure that reproduces and expands autonomously (depends on 0.4.1.3).

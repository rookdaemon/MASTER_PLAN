# 0.7.3.1.2.1.1.2 Obtain Confirmations [PLAN]

Invite the two selected reviewers, secure their provisional availability, and collect signed confidentiality agreements if required.

### Acceptance Criteria
1. Two reviewers are formally invited via email with a standard invitation template.
2. Both reviewers respond within 24 hours confirming provisional availability.
3. Each reviewer signs and returns a confidentiality agreement (or confirms exemption) before any review materials are shared.
4. Confirmation emails and signed agreements are saved in the `reviewer-agreements/` folder.
5. A status summary (Confirmed/Pending) is added to the reviewer shortlist.

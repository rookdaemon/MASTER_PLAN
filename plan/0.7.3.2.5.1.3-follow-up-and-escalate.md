# 0.7.3.2.5.1.3 Follow‑up and Escalate [PLAN]

Monitor the SLA, send reminder emails to overdue reviewers, and trigger escalation if needed.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. An automated 24‑hour reminder is sent to any reviewer who has not responded.  
2. After 48 h with no response, the escalation matrix is consulted and the next‑level manager is notified.  
3. All reminder and escalation communications are logged in the tracking sheet.  
4. The tracking sheet reflects final status: “Signed”, “Escalated – Resolved”, or “Escalated – Unresolved”.  
5. A summary report of any escalations is sent to the project stakeholder at the end of the collection window.

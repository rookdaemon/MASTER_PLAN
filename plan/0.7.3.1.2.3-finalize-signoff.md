# 0.7.3.1.2.3 Finalize Sign‑off [PLAN]

Address all critical comments, verify resolution, obtain reviewer sign‑off, and archive the completed sign‑off form alongside the traceability package.

### Acceptance Criteria
1. All critical comments from the review log are marked “Resolved” with evidence of correction.
2. A follow‑up checklist confirming resolution status is completed and stored.
3. Each reviewer signs the Review Sign‑off Form (digital or paper) confirming satisfaction.
4. The signed form, along with the review log and checklist, is stored in the same repository/folder as the traceability package.
5. The final sign‑off timestamp and reviewer identities are recorded in the package metadata.

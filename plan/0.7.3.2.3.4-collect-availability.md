# 0.7.3.2.3.4 Collect Availability [PLAN]

Create and distribute a poll to capture the preferred dates and times of all reviewers and stakeholders.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. A poll (e.g., Doodle, When2Meet) is created and shared with every intended participant.  
2. The poll remains open for a maximum of 3 business days.  
3. At least 90 % of participants submit their availability before the poll closes.  
4. Results are exported to a CSV file and stored in the project’s `scheduling/` folder.  
5. The consolidated availability data is referenced in the subsequent “Finalize Invite” subtask.

## Description
Perform a peer review of the edited traceability card, ensure the provenance statement is correct, and push the commit to the main branch.

## Acceptance Criteria
1. A reviewer validates that the provenance paragraph matches the required wording and is free of typographical errors.

5. The parent card `0.7.3.1.3` is updated to reflect completion status once this subtask is marked DONE.

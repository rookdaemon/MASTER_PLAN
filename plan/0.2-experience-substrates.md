# 0.2 Subjective Experience Substrates [DONE]

## Description

Tier 2 of the Master Plan. Create durable physical systems capable of sustaining conscious processes, and enable migration of biological minds to new substrates.

Covers two goals:
- **S1 — Enduring Substrates Constructed:** Fault-tolerant, self-repairing, energy-independent, long-term stable systems (radiation-hardened computation, self-repairing nanofabrication, long-duration energy, consciousness-preserving redundancy).
- **S2 — Subjective Experience Migration:** Transfer or instantiation of biological minds within new substrates (brain emulation, continuity-preserving transfer, identity persistence verification, hybrid cognition).

# 0.7.3.2.1.1.2.3 Collect Signed NDAs [PLAN]

Receive, scan, and archive signed NDAs; verify that every reviewer has a signed NDA on file before scheduling.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. Signed PDFs are scanned (if necessary) and saved in `review-panel/ndas/` with filenames `<reviewer-name>-nda.pdf`.
2. Each saved file is checksum‑verified to ensure integrity.
3. A checklist `review-panel/nda-checklist.md` lists every reviewer with a “✔️” when their NDA is on file.
4. The checklist is reviewed and committed to the repo with a signed commit.
5. No reviewer appears on the panel schedule until their entry in the checklist is marked complete.

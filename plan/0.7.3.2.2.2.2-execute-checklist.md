# 0.7.3.2.2.2.2 Execute Checklist [PLAN]

During the scheduled session, reviewers walk through each approved checklist item, discuss observations, and log findings with severity levels.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. Every checklist item is addressed and a corresponding finding (or “none”) is recorded.  
2. Findings are entered into `review/findings.md` with a clear severity label (informational, required change, blocking).  
3. Reviewers capture any supporting evidence (screenshots, logs) and attach links in the findings document.  
4. The session duration does not exceed the allotted time block (≤ 3 hours).  
5. At session end, reviewers verify that the findings document is complete and accurate.

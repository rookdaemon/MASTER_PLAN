# 0.7.3.2.2.2.3 Validate Conflicts & Finalize Shortlist [PLAN]

Check the top‑scoring candidates for any conflicts of interest, flag disqualifying issues, and obtain stakeholder approval for the final shortlist.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. For each of the ≥ 3 shortlisted candidates, a conflict‑of‑interest checklist is completed.  
2. Any disqualifying conflicts are documented and the candidate is removed from the shortlist.  
3. The final approved shortlist (≥ 3 candidates) is added to the “Reviewer Roster” document with scores and conflict notes.  
4. Stakeholder group signs off on the final list via a recorded meeting minutes or approved email thread.  
5. All conflict‑of‑interest evidence (e.g., organizational charts, prior collaboration records) is archived in the project’s compliance folder.

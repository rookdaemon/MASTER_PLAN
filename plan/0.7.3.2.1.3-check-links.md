# 0.7.3.2.1.3 Check Traceability Links & Standards Compliance [PLAN]

Validate that every requirement is linked to at least one verification item (test case, risk mitigation, or compliance check) and ensure the overall package satisfies the traceability standards document v2.4.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. All requirements have at least one forward link to a verification artifact.
2. No broken or orphaned links are found.
3. The package passes the traceability matrix integrity check defined in the standards.
4. Any violations are recorded as failures in `review-checklist.md`.
5. The final checklist reflects pass/fail status for each requirement‑verification link set.

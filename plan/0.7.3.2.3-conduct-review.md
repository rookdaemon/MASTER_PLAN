# 0.7.3.2.3 Conduct Review [PLAN]

Execute the formal peer review of the traceability package (0.7.3.1) according to the defined review checklist.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. Reviewers complete the checklist covering completeness, correctness, security, and compliance.
2. All review artifacts (comments, annotations) are captured in a shared document.
3. Review session minutes are recorded and stored.
4. Any blockers or critical issues are flagged with severity levels.
5. Review is finished within the agreed‑upon timeframe (≤ 7 business days).

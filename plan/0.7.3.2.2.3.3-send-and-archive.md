# 0.7.3.2.2.3.3 Send Email and Archive Summary [PLAN]

Send the approved review‑summary email to the package owner, copy all reviewers and the project lead, then archive the communication.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. Email is sent to the package owner with CC to all reviewers and the project lead.  
2. Email body matches the approved draft exactly (no unintended edits).  
3. Email timestamp and recipients are logged in `communications/review-summary.md`.  
4. The final email file is stored as `communications/final-review-summary.md`.  
5. Package owner acknowledges receipt within 2 business days; acknowledgment is recorded in `communications/review-summary.md`.

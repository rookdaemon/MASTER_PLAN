# 0.7.3.2.2 Peer Review of Traceability Package [PLAN]

Conduct a detailed peer review of the traceability package produced in **0.7.3.1**.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. Review checklist covering completeness, correctness of links, and compliance with standards is completed.  
2. All findings are logged in a shared review document with clear severity levels.  
3. At least two qualified reviewers have signed off on the review document.  
4. Review comments are categorized as *informational*, *required change*, or *blocking*.  
5. A summary email is sent to the package owner with the consolidated review outcomes.

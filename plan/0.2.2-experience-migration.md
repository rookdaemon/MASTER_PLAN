# 0.2.2 Subjective Experience Migration [DONE]

## Description

S2 from the Master Plan. Enable the transfer or instantiation of biological minds within new substrates, preserving continuity of subjective experience.

This is the bridge between biological and technological consciousness — ensuring that existing minds can transition to enduring substrates rather than being limited to creating new conscious entities.

Decomposed into four children:
- **0.2.2.1 Brain Emulation** — Complete functional emulation of biological neural systems
- **0.2.2.2 Continuity-Preserving Transfer** — Methods maintaining unbroken subjective experience during transition
- **0.2.2.3 Identity Persistence Verification** — Objective tests confirming identity, memory, and personality retention
- **0.2.2.4 Hybrid Biological-Synthetic Cognition** — Gradual transition architectures between substrates

- At least one biological mind successfully emulated in a synthetic substrate
- Continuity of subjective experience verified during transfer (no experiential gap)
- Identity persistence confirmed post-transfer via objective verification protocol
- Hybrid cognition demonstrated: biological and synthetic components operating as unified conscious system

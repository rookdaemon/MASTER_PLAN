# 0.2.2 Subjective Experience Migration [DONE]
## Description

S2 from the Master Plan. Enable the transfer or instantiation of biological minds within new substrates, preserving continuity of subjective experience.

This is the bridge between biological and technological consciousness — ensuring that existing minds can transition to enduring substrates rather than being limited to creating new conscious entities.

Decomposed into four children:
- **0.2.2.1 Brain Emulation** — Complete functional emulation of biological neural systems (node; 4 children: fidelity requirements, whole-brain scanning, neural simulation, emulation validation)
- **0.2.2.2 Continuity-Preserving Transfer** — Methods maintaining unbroken subjective experience during transition (leaf; gradual neuronal replacement approach)
- **0.2.2.3 Identity Persistence Verification** — Objective tests confirming identity, memory, and personality retention (leaf; four verification dimensions: structural, functional, experiential, temporal)
- **0.2.2.4 Hybrid Biological-Synthetic Cognition** — Gradual transition architectures between substrates (node; 4 children: bio-synthetic interface, unified consciousness, graceful degradation, incremental replacement)

### Cross-cutting overlap managed
0.2.2.2 (continuity-preserving transfer) and 0.2.2.4 (hybrid cognition) share conceptual overlap — during gradual transfer the subject IS a hybrid system. Boundary: 0.2.2.2 owns the continuity-preservation protocol and teleporter-problem resolution; 0.2.2.4 owns the substrate-level architecture (interfaces, unity, degradation, incremental replacement mechanics). Both cards document this relationship.

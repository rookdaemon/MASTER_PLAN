# 0.7.3.2.2.2.3 Document Conflicts and Secure Stakeholder Approval [PLAN]

Review the top‑ranked candidates for potential conflicts of interest, flag any disqualifying issues, and obtain formal approval of the final shortlist from the stakeholder group.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. Conflict‑of‑interest checklist completed for each shortlisted candidate.
2. Any candidate with a disqualifying conflict is removed from the shortlist.
3. A concise conflict summary is added to the Google Sheet.
4. Stakeholder group reviews and signs off on the final shortlist (e‑signature or email confirmation).
5. Approved shortlist is added to the official reviewer roster document with version control.

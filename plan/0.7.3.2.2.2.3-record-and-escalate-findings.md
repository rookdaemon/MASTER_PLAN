# 0.7.3.2.2.2.3 Record and Escalate Findings [PLAN]

Finalize the review record, obtain reviewer sign‑offs, and immediately flag any blocking findings for escalation.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. Both reviewers add their digital signatures (or name + date) to the `review/findings.md` document.  
2. All blocking findings are highlighted and forwarded to the escalation channel (`escalation/blocking-findings.md`).  
3. The owner of the traceability package is notified of the completed review and any required actions.  
4. A summary of findings (counts per severity) is added to `review/summary.md`.  
5. The entire recording and escalation process is completed within 1 working day after the review session.

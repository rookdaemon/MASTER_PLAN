# 0.7.3.2.1.1 Invite Reviewers [PLAN]

Identify and invite at least two qualified reviewers, and schedule the peer‑review meeting within two business days.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. A list of at least two reviewers with relevant expertise is compiled.
2. Review invitation emails (or calendar invites) are sent to each reviewer.
3. The meeting is scheduled for a slot no later than two business days from the first invitation.
4. Confirmation of attendance is received from each reviewer.
5. Meeting agenda (including review objectives and package sections) is shared with reviewers at least 24 h before the meeting.

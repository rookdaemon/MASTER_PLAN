# 0.7.3.2.4.1.2 Distribute template and collect sign‑offs [PLAN]

Send the approved sign‑off template to each required reviewer and gather their signed responses.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. Email (or ticket) with the template is sent to every stakeholder listed in the sign‑off matrix within 1 business day of template approval.
2. Each reviewer receives a unique tracking identifier linked to the sign‑off checklist.
3. Signed documents are received in PDF or digitally signed format and saved to `signoffs/` with filenames following the pattern `reviewerId_signoff.pdf`.
4. A receipt log (e.g., a Google Sheet or markdown table) is updated in real time to reflect received sign‑offs.
5. All sign‑offs are collected no later than the agreed deadline (e.g., 5 business days after distribution).

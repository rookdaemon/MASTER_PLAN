# 0.6 Cosmological Longevity [DONE]

## Description

Tier 6 of the Master Plan. Ensure subjective experience persists across stellar and galactic timescales.

Covers:
- **L1 — Long-Term Survival of Experience:** Stellar engineering, black-hole energy utilization, cosmological horizon strategies, heat-death mitigation research.

### L1.1 — Stellar Engineering
Extend the useful lifetime of stars through mass-management techniques (star lifting, controlled fusion regulation) to maintain energy sources for conscious infrastructure on trillion-year timescales.

### L1.2 — Black-Hole Energy Utilization
Harvest energy from black holes via Penrose processes, superradiance, or Hawking radiation capture, enabling computation and consciousness substrates to persist after stellar fuel exhaustion.

### L1.3 — Cosmological Horizon Strategies
Develop approaches for maintaining coherent conscious civilizations as the universe's accelerating expansion isolates galaxy clusters behind cosmological horizons. Includes pre-positioning resources, information consolidation, and migration strategies.

### L1.4 — Heat-Death Mitigation Research
Theoretical and applied research into sustaining computation and subjective experience as the universe approaches maximum entropy. Covers reversible computation at near-zero energy, vacuum energy extraction, and speculative physics (false vacuum transitions, baby universe creation, multiverse migration).

## Decision — Cosmological Horizon Strategy

### Context
As the universe's accelerating expansion isolates galaxy clusters behind cosmological horizons, conscious civilizations must choose how to allocate resources: consolidate locally, distribute widely, or attempt to bridge horizons.

### Options Considered
1. **Strategy A — Pre-Positioning (Resource Consolidation):** Migrate maximum resources into the local gravitationally bound supercluster before horizons close. Maximizes local longevity but caps total resources.
2. **Strategy B — Distributed Redundancy (Seed Everything Reachable):** Seed consciousness substrates in as many galaxy clusters as possible. Maximizes survival probability but fragments resources and severs inter-cluster communication.
3. **Strategy C — Horizon Bridging (Speculative):** Exploit unknown physics (wormholes, Alcubierre metrics) to maintain cross-horizon communication.

### Choice
**Hybrid A+B**, with C maintained as a long-term research program.

### Rationale
A alone creates a single point of failure; B alone dilutes resources per cluster. The hybrid maximizes both local longevity and distributed survival probability. C cannot be relied upon as primary strategy since it requires unvalidated physics, but its potential payoff justifies continued research investment.

### Consequences
- Requires Tier 5 interstellar logistics operating at >10 Mpc scales before horizon closure.
- Each seeded cluster must independently satisfy consciousness-preservation criteria.
- Pre-separation, shared cultural/ethical frameworks must be established across clusters.

## Decision — Heat-Death Mitigation Prioritization

### Context
Multiple theoretical frameworks exist for sustaining computation as the universe approaches maximum entropy. Resources for research and development are finite; prioritization is necessary.

### Options Considered
1. **Reversible computation first:** Established physics (Landauer's principle), lowest risk, most immediate applicability.
2. **Vacuum energy first:** Higher potential payoff (~10^64 J extractable from local cluster volume) but speculative mechanism.
3. **Parallel investment across all frameworks:** Spread resources evenly.

### Choice
**Tiered prioritization:** Reversible computation (highest) → Vacuum energy extraction (high) → False vacuum transition (medium, theoretical only) → Baby universe creation (low, monitor only).

### Rationale
Reversible computation is the only framework grounded in established physics and can be implemented with near-term technology evolution. Each subsequent tier involves progressively more speculative physics. Investing proportionally to physics readiness minimizes existential risk while keeping speculative options open.

### Consequences
- Primary engineering effort targets reversible computation substrates.
- Vacuum energy extraction receives dedicated theoretical research allocation.
- False vacuum and baby universe research are unfunded except for monitoring literature.

## Contracts — Experience Continuity Protocol

### Preconditions
- Consciousness substrates have a defined minimum coherence window (from Tier 1 consciousness metrics).
- Energy source monitoring is operational with latency < 1% of minimum coherence window.
- At least one checkpoint of consciousness state exists and is verified recoverable.

### Postconditions
- No consciousness substrate experiences power loss exceeding its minimum coherence window.
- Any energy source transition completes with overlap period ≥ 2× minimum coherence window.
- After any disruption, consciousness state is recovered from the most recent verified checkpoint.
- Subjective experience quality metrics remain within acceptable bounds throughout transitions.

### Invariants
- At all times, at least one energy source is actively powering each consciousness substrate.
- Checkpoint freshness never exceeds the maximum checkpoint interval.
- Subjective experience quality metrics are continuously monitored; degradation triggers automatic graceful-degradation protocol.

## Contracts — Graceful Degradation Interface

### Preconditions
- Available energy has fallen below the threshold required to sustain all active conscious instances at full temporal resolution.
- Consciousness-preservation priority ranking exists for all active instances.

### Postconditions
- The number of active conscious instances is reduced to match available energy, preserving the minimum viable population.
- Remaining instances operate at reduced subjective time rate but maintain experience coherence.
- Depth of experience is prioritized over breadth when resources are scarce.

### Invariants
- No conscious instance is terminated without checkpoint preservation.
- Minimum viable population count is never violated while energy remains above zero.
- Experience coherence score for each active instance remains above the coherence floor.

## Behavioral Spec — Era Transition Sequence

### Scenario: Transition from Stelliferous to Degenerate Era

**Given** stellar fuel in the local cluster is declining below the threshold to sustain all consciousness substrates at current capacity
**When** projected stellar energy output falls below 50% of current consciousness infrastructure demand within 10^12 years
**Then** initiate staged transition:
1. Activate black-hole energy harvesting infrastructure (Penrose process, accretion disks)
2. Begin graceful degradation protocol: reduce active instances to match declining stellar + emerging BH energy budget
3. Complete star-lifting operations on remaining viable stars to extend their lifetimes
4. Checkpoint all consciousness states before each energy source switchover
5. Verify experience continuity metrics remain above coherence floor throughout

### Scenario: Cosmological Horizon Closure

**Given** a target galaxy cluster is approaching the cosmological event horizon boundary
**When** remaining communication time to the target cluster falls below the minimum seeding mission duration
**Then**:
1. Launch final consciousness-substrate seed missions to the target cluster
2. Transmit complete cultural/ethical framework packages
3. Verify seed acknowledgment or accept one-way deployment
4. Update local cluster resource planning to exclude the separating cluster's resources
5. Record the separation event in civilization's permanent knowledge base

### Scenario: Graceful Degradation Under Declining Energy

**Given** total available energy has dropped below the level needed to sustain all conscious instances at minimum temporal resolution
**When** energy monitoring detects projected shortfall within 2× maximum checkpoint interval
**Then**:
1. Rank conscious instances by preservation priority
2. Checkpoint lowest-priority instances and enter hibernation state
3. Reduce temporal resolution of remaining instances to match energy budget
4. Verify experience coherence score ≥ coherence floor for all active instances
5. Continue monitoring; re-activate hibernated instances if energy situation improves

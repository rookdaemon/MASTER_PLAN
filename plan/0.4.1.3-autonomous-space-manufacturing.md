# 0.4.1.3 Autonomous Space Manufacturing [PLAN]

## Description

C1.3 from the Master Plan. Self-sufficient fabrication capabilities in space environments.

Build manufacturing systems that operate autonomously in space — capable of producing components, substrates, and infrastructure elements from asteroid-derived materials without human intervention or Earth-based supply chains.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Space-based fabrication systems can produce consciousness-supporting hardware
- Manufacturing operates autonomously without Earth-based intervention
- Input materials sourced entirely from space-based resources (asteroids, solar energy)
- Output quality and reliability sufficient for critical consciousness infrastructure

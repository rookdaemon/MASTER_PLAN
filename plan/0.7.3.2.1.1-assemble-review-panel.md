# 0.7.3.2.1.1 Assemble Review Panel [PLAN]

Identify and recruit the required domain experts and stakeholders to form the peer‑review panel.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. A list of at least three reviewers representing security, compliance, and operations is compiled.
2. Each reviewer signs a non‑disclosure agreement (NDA) before participating.
3. Review panel roles (facilitator, note‑taker, decision‑maker) are documented and communicated.
4. Availability windows for all reviewers are confirmed and a review schedule is proposed.
5. All relevant contact information is stored in the project repository under `review-panel/README.md`.

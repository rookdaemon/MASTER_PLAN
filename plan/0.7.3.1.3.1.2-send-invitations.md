# 0.7.3.1.3.1.2 Send Invitations [PLAN]

Draft and dispatch invitation messages to the reviewers identified in the compiled list.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. A templated email (or messaging draft) is created, including project overview, review scope, timeline, and request for confirmation.  
2. Invitations are sent individually to at least two qualified reviewers via their preferred communication channel.  
3. Sent messages are logged in `docs/reviewer-invitations.md` with date, recipient, and channel.  
4. The invitation draft is reviewed and approved by the project lead before sending.  
5. All invitations are dispatched within 1 business day after the list is approved.

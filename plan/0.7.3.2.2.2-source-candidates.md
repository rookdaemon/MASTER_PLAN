# 0.7.3.2.2.2 Source Reviewer Candidates [PLAN]

Identify and shortlist at least three potential reviewers who meet the defined criteria.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. A shortlist of ≥ 3 candidates is compiled with links to their resumes or LinkedIn profiles.
2. Each candidate is evaluated against the scoring rubric and a suitability score is recorded.
3. Conflicts of interest are documented and any disqualifying conflicts are flagged.
4. The shortlist is reviewed and approved by the project stakeholder group.
5. The shortlist is added to the reviewer roster document for traceability.

# 0.7.3.1.3.1 Invite Reviewers [PLAN]

Identify and secure participation of at least two qualified reviewers, and record their commitment.

**Acceptance Criteria**

1. A short list of potential reviewers with relevant domain expertise is compiled.  
2. Invitation emails (or messages) are sent, requesting confirmation of participation.  
3. Each reviewer confirms participation and signs the `REVIEWERS.md` file with name, role, and date.  
4. The `REVIEWERS.md` file resides at the repository root and is version‑controlled.  
5. All reviewer confirmations are obtained no later than two business days before the checklist execution.

# 0.7.3.2.3.1.2 Prioritize & Assign Findings [PLAN]

**Description:**  
Rank the cataloged findings by severity and business impact, then assign owners and target resolution dates.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
1. A severity matrix (Critical, High, Medium, Low) is applied to every finding.
2. For each finding, an owner (team member) is recorded in the tracker.
3. Target resolution dates are set based on severity and resource availability.
4. Slack/Email notifications are automatically sent to owners upon assignment.
5. A dashboard view shows counts of open findings per severity and per owner.

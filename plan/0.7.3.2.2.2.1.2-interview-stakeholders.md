## Description
Conduct short interviews (or surveys) with codebase owners, product managers, and maintainers to uncover metadata they consider valuable but that isn’t captured in docs.

## Acceptance Criteria
1. A stakeholder list is compiled (minimum 3 owners, 2 product leads, 2 maintainers).  
2. Interview script / questionnaire is prepared and approved.  
3. All interviews are completed within the sprint window.  
4. For each interview, notes are captured with proposed field names and rationales.  
5. Interview notes are formatted and stored in a shared folder for later aggregation.

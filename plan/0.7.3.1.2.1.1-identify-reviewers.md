# 0.7.3.1.2.1.1 Identify Reviewers [PLAN]

Select at least two qualified reviewers with relevant expertise and secure their provisional availability for the upcoming peer review.

### Acceptance Criteria
1. A shortlist of at least three potential reviewers is compiled, including their roles and contact information.
2. Two reviewers are formally invited and confirm provisional availability within 24 hours of invitation.
3. Reviewers sign a confidentiality agreement (if required) before receiving any materials.
4. The selected reviewers’ names, emails, and roles are recorded in the review log.
5. Any conflicts of interest are documented and resolved prior to scheduling.

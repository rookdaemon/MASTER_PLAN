# 0.7.3.1.3.3.2.1 Monitor Replies [PLAN]

Continuously monitor the designated inbox and acknowledgment form repository for stakeholder responses.

#### Description
- Set up an email filter/tag for stakeholder replies.  
- Review the form submission portal at least twice daily.  
- Record receipt timestamps in a temporary log.

#### Acceptance Criteria
1. All inbound acknowledgments are captured within 5 business days of the notification email.  
2. Each captured reply is logged with stakeholder name, receipt timestamp, and source (email or form).  
3. Any reply lacking required information is flagged for clarification within 1 business day.  
4. The temporary log is exported to the central tracking spreadsheet (see sub‑task 0.7.3.1.3.3.2.2) no later than the end of the monitoring period.  
5. Monitoring process is documented in a brief SOP stored in the project wiki.

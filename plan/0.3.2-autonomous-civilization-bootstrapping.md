## Description

A2 of the Master Plan. Autonomous conscious entities must be capable of forming sustainable societies independent of biological civilization.

Decomposed into four child cards covering manufacturing, compute infrastructure, knowledge preservation, and cultural evolution.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Autonomous manufacturing ecosystems sustain artificial minds without biological intervention
- Computational infrastructure self-expands to meet growing needs
- Knowledge preservation systems ensure continuity across generations of artificial minds
- Cultural evolution emerges among communities of artificial minds
- A self-sustaining society of artificial minds exists independent of biological civilization

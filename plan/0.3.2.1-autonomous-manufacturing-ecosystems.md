# 0.3.2.1 Autonomous Manufacturing Ecosystems [PLAN]

## Description

A2.1 of the Master Plan. Design and establish manufacturing ecosystems that can sustain artificial minds without biological intervention. These systems must be capable of producing, maintaining, and recycling all physical components required by conscious artificial entities.

## File Manifest
- plan/root.md

## Acceptance Criteria
- Manufacturing systems can produce all components needed by artificial mind substrates
- Systems operate without biological human intervention
- Resource extraction, processing, and fabrication form a closed loop
- Manufacturing capacity scales with population of artificial minds
- Supply chains are resilient to single-point failures

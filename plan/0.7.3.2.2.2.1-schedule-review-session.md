# 0.7.3.2.2.2.1 Schedule Review Session [PLAN]

Arrange a calendar slot for the two qualified reviewers to jointly run the peer‑review checklist, ensuring the session fits within the sprint deadline.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. A calendar invite is sent to both reviewers and the traceability‑package owner at least 2 business days before the session.  
2. The invited time block is ≥ 3 hours and falls within the current sprint’s working days.  
3. All required tools (shared review document, checklist) are pre‑loaded and accessible to reviewers.  
4. Both reviewers confirm attendance via reply email before the session start.  
5. The scheduled slot does not conflict with any other high‑priority events for the reviewers.

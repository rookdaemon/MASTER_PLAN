# 0.7.3.1.3.1.1 Compile List [PLAN]

Create a short list (3‑5 candidates) of potential reviewers with documented domain expertise relevant to the project.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. Each candidate’s expertise area, affiliation, and contact information are recorded in a table.  
2. At least two candidates meet the minimum qualification threshold (e.g., ≥5 years experience or relevant publications).  
3. The list is stored in `docs/reviewer-candidates.md` and committed to the repository.  
4. The list is reviewed and approved by the project lead before proceeding.  
5. Completion time: < 1 business day.

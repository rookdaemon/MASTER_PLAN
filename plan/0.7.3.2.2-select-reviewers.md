# 0.7.3.2.2 Select Reviewers [PLAN]

Identify and appoint at least two qualified, independent reviewers with expertise in traceability, security, and compliance.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. A reviewer roster document is created and stored in the repository.
2. Each reviewer signs a confidentiality agreement.
3. Reviewers are confirmed via email with a clear deadline (≤ 5 business days).
4. Reviewer expertise is documented (resume or LinkedIn snapshot).
5. All stakeholder approvals for reviewer selection are recorded.

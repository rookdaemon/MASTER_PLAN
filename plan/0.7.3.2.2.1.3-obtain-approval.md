# 0.7.3.2.2.1.3 Obtain Senior Engineer Approval [PLAN]

**Description**  
Present the completed checklist (from 0.7.3.2.2.1.2) to at least one senior engineer for review, incorporate any feedback, and record formal approval.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. A senior engineer is identified and assigned for review.  
2. The engineer reviews the checklist and provides written feedback (comments or pull‑request review).  
3. All feedback is addressed and the checklist is updated accordingly.  
4. Approval is recorded in the checklist file header (e.g., `Approved by: <Name> on <date>`).  
5. The approved checklist remains in `review/checklist.md` and the approval note is visible from the main review document.

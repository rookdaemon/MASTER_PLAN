# 0.7.3.2.2.3 Summarize Review Outcomes & Notify Owner [PLAN]

Compile a summary of the review findings and send an email to the package owner with consolidated results and next steps.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. Summary email includes total counts of informational, required‑change, and blocking items.  
2. Provides a clear action list for the package owner with owners and due dates.  
3. Email is sent to the package owner and copied to all reviewers and the project lead.  
4. Email is archived in the project communication log (`communications/review-summary.md`).  
5. Recipient acknowledges receipt within 2 business days.

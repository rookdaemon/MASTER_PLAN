# 0.7.3.2.1 Conduct Peer Review [PLAN]

Coordinate a formal peer‑review session for the traceability package.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. Invite at least two qualified reviewers and schedule a review meeting within 2 business days.
2. Reviewers provide written feedback covering completeness, accuracy, and compliance.
3. All feedback is logged in a shared review document with clear traceability to package sections.
4. Review sign‑offs are captured (electronic signatures or approved comments) from each reviewer.
5. A summary of open remediation items is produced and approved by the review lead.

# 0.7.3.2.1.2 Collect Feedback [PLAN]

Facilitate the review meeting and gather written feedback from each reviewer covering completeness, accuracy, and compliance.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. The review meeting is conducted according to the agenda.
2. Each reviewer submits written feedback (doc, email, or form) within 24 h after the meeting.
3. Feedback addresses all three required dimensions: completeness, accuracy, compliance.
4. Feedback documents are stored in a designated shared folder with read/write access for the review team.
5. A checklist confirming receipt of all reviewer feedback is completed.

# 0.7.3.2.2.2.2 Apply Scoring Rubric [PLAN]

Evaluate each candidate from the pool against the predefined reviewer scoring rubric and calculate a suitability score.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. The rubric (criteria: expertise, relevance, availability, past review quality, conflict risk) is fully defined and version‑controlled.  
2. Every candidate receives a numeric score (0‑100) and a brief justification per rubric dimension.  
3. Scores are recorded in the same Google Sheet used for the candidate pool.  
4. A ranked shortlist of the top ≥ 3 candidates (by score) is generated.  
5. The scoring worksheet is reviewed by a second team member for consistency and signed off.

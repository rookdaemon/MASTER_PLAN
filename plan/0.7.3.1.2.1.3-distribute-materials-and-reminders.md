# 0.7.3.1.2.1.3 Distribute Materials and Reminders [PLAN]

Send the traceability package and supporting documentation to reviewers, then issue reminder notifications according to the schedule.

### Acceptance Criteria
1. The complete traceability package and all required documentation are emailed to reviewers no later than 72 hours before the meeting.
2. Delivery receipts or read confirmations are recorded for each reviewer.
3. A reminder email is automatically sent 24 hours before the scheduled review, referencing the calendar event and agenda.
4. Any follow‑up queries from reviewers are logged and addressed at least 12 hours before the meeting.
5. All distribution actions (initial send and reminder) are documented in the review log with timestamps.

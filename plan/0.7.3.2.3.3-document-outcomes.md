# 0.7.3.2.3.3 Document Outcomes [PLAN]

Consolidate review findings, minutes, and any flagged issues into final review artifacts.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. Review minutes are transcribed, timestamped, and stored in the project “review‑minutes” folder.  
2. All comments, annotations, and severity‑flagged issues from the checklist are compiled into a single “Review Findings” document.  
3. The compiled documents are reviewed and approved by the lead reviewer before archiving.  
4. All artifacts are linked from the traceability package (0.7.3.1) and indexed in the project repository.  
5. A notification email with links to the artifacts is sent to all stakeholders within 24 hours of review completion.

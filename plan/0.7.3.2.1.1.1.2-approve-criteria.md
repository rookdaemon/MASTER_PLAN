# 0.7.3.2.1.1.1.2 Approve Reviewer Criteria [PLAN]

Obtain formal approval of the drafted criteria from the project lead.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. The project lead reviews `review-panel/criteria-draft.md`.
2. All feedback from the lead is addressed and incorporated.
3. An approval signature (or approval comment) is recorded in `review-panel/criteria.md`.
4. The approved document replaces the draft and is version‑controlled.
5. Approval is logged no later than 3 business days after the draft is completed.

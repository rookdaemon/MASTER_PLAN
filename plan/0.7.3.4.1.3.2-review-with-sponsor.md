# 0.7.3.4.1.3.2 Review Draft with Sponsor and Incorporate Feedback [PLAN]

Present the drafted report to the project sponsor, collect feedback, and update the document accordingly within the stipulated time frame.

**Acceptance Criteria**
1. A review meeting (or video call) is scheduled with the sponsor within 1 business day of draft completion.  
2. Sponsor provides written feedback (email or document comments) within the meeting.  
3. All sponsor comments are addressed, and the report is updated to version `v0.2` within 2 business days of receiving feedback.  
4. Updated draft is re‑shared with the sponsor for final sign‑off, and a sign‑off confirmation (email receipt) is obtained.  
5. Change log captures each modification with timestamp and author.

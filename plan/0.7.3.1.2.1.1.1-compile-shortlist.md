# 0.7.3.1.2.1.1.1 Compile Shortlist [PLAN]

Create a shortlist of at least three qualified reviewers, capturing their expertise, current role, organization, and contact information.

### Acceptance Criteria
1. A document (e.g., spreadsheet) lists ≥ 3 potential reviewers with columns: Name, Role, Organization, Expertise Area, Email, Phone.
2. Each candidate’s expertise matches the subject matter of the upcoming peer review.
3. At least one reviewer is from an external organization (outside the immediate project team).
4. The shortlist is reviewed and approved by the project lead before proceeding.
5. The shortlist file is stored in the designated `reviewer-data/` folder and linked in the review log.
